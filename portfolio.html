Chef's Specials

Our chef's special creations that showcase the finest ingredients and culinary expertise.

Signature Butter Chicken

Chef's Special

Our signature butter chicken is prepared using a traditional recipe with a modern twist. Tender chicken pieces are marinated overnight, then cooked in a clay oven before being simmered in a rich, creamy tomato sauce with a blend of aromatic spices.

Chef Rajesh Kumar

Head Chef

Royal Paneer Masala

Chef's Special

Our royal paneer masala features house-made cottage cheese cubes in a luxurious gravy of cashews, tomatoes, and aromatic spices. The dish is finished with a touch of cream and fresh coriander for a truly royal experience.

Chef Priya Sharma

Vegetarian Specialist

Artisanal Tiramisu

Chef's Special

Our artisanal tiramisu is a modern interpretation of the classic Italian dessert. Layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers are alternated with a light, airy mascarpone cream, dusted with premium cocoa powder, and garnished with chocolate shavings.

Chef Marco Rossi

Pastry Chef

Garlic Butter Prawns

Chef's Special

Our garlic butter prawns feature jumbo prawns sautéed in a rich garlic butter sauce with white wine and fresh herbs. Served with crusty bread to soak up the delicious sauce, this dish is a seafood lover's delight.

Chef Lisa Chen

Seafood Specialist
